CVS Libraries Installation
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Smarty - PHP Template
---------------------
1. Download Smarty
   http://smarty.php.net/
2. Unpack archive
3. copy 'libs' to '_source/tier.lib'
3. rename 'libs' to 'smarty'


Creole - Database Access Layer
-------------------------------
1. Download Creole
   http://creole.phpdb.org/wiki/
2. Unpack archive
3. Copy 'creole' and 'jargon' from 'classes/'
   into '_source/tier.lib'
